Weather-Based Recommendations

Sunny Days

Enjoy the beautiful weather by visiting Kyoto’s outdoor attractions such as the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Fushimi Inari Shrine, and Kiyomizu-dera. Don’t forget your sunscreen and a hat!

Rainy Days

Explore Kyoto’s indoor activities like the Kyoto National Museum, Gion District, and traditional tea houses. Bring an umbrella and dress in layers to stay comfortable.

Cold Days

Warm up by visiting Kyoto’s cozy cafes and hot spring baths. Try a visit to the Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) for a picturesque winter scene, and dress warmly to enjoy the outdoors.

Hot Days

Stay cool with a visit to Kyoto’s riverbanks or enjoy an early morning visit to places like the Kyoto Imperial Palace. Keep hydrated and wear light clothing to stay comfortable.
